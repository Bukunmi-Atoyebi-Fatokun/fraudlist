What do you do if you just got scammed?

  1. Step 1- Block all access with scammer.
  2. Step 2-Secure your details and download a copy of all your conversations or transactions and put it in a PDF.
  3. Step 3- Send an email with an attached copy of all evidence to the scammer account, and copy your bank, EFCC, CBN, and whistleblower.
  4. Step 4- Send an e-mail to the banks or email dedicated to reporting scam cases.
  5. Step 5- Report to your Bank for subsequent actions
  6. Step 6 -Visit the Scammers Bank branch nearest to your location with a printed copy of all conversation details.
